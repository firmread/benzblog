CV

Benz Thanachart Siripatrachai is a filmmaker and photographer from Thailand.

Benz was one of the assistant directors of the feature film “Uncle Boonmee Who Can Recalls His Past Lives” by Apichatpong Weerasetakul, Palme d’Or prize at the 2010 Cannes Film Festival.

Benz currently resides in New York City, pursuing his Master Degree at School of Visual Arts.
